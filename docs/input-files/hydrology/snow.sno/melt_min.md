# melt_min

<!-- Source: https://swatplus.gitbook.io/io-docs/introduction-1/hydrology/snow.sno/melt_min -->

The variables [melt\_max](melt_max.md) and *melt\_min* allow the rate of snow melt to vary through the year and account for the impact of snowpack density on snow melt.

If the watershed is in the Northern Hemisphere, *melt\_min* will be the maximum melt factor. If the watershed is in the Southern Hemisphere, *melt\_min* will be the minimum melt factor.

# melt_max

<!-- Source: https://swatplus.gitbook.io/io-docs/introduction-1/hydrology/snow.sno/melt_max -->

The variables *melt\_max* and [melt\_min](melt_min.md) allow the rate of snow melt to vary through the year and account for the impact of snowpack density on snow melt.

If the watershed is in the Northern Hemisphere, *melt\_max* will be the maximum melt factor. If the watershed is in the Southern Hemisphere, *melt\_max* will be the minimum melt factor.

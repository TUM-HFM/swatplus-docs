# Soils

<!-- Source: https://swatplus.gitbook.io/io-docs/introduction-1/soils -->

The physical soil properties are summarized in the file [**soils.sol**](soils.sol.md). The physical properties of the soil govern the movement of water and air through the profile and have a major impact on the cycling of water within the HRU.

The contents of nutrients and constituents in the soil are initialized using the file [**soil\_plant.ini**](../initialization/soil_plant.ini.md), which references the files [**nutrients.sol**](nutrients.sol.md) for the initial nutrient contents and the files [**pest\_hru.ini**](../initialization/pest_hru.ini.md) and [**salt\_hru.ini**](../initialization/salt_hru.ini.md) for the initial contents of pesticides and salts in the soil.
